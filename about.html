our story

We believe in the unique power of art in transforming lives. Rooted in our founder’s personal experience with a life-changing masterpiece, the gallery’s mission in sharing happiness with art was born. The gallery is housed in the conserved colonial barracks of Gilman Barracks—one of the pioneer contemporary arts cluster in Singapore. Our work focuses on contemporary art and develops privileged relationships with international artists, whose works are presented in various forms of media including drawing, painting, sculpture, photography and digital animation. We also provide art consultancy for various corporations and hold events for a wide variety of clients.

mission & vision
mission

Bringing the finest quality of art by new talented artists from across the globe to promote their works via domestic and international art markets.

vision

To unleash emerging artists' capability to realize their greatest creative potential in proposing alternatives to current realities.

the team

Aluna George

Gallery Director

Aluna works with artists and galleries from all over the world, sourcing and commissioning artworks for private and corporate clients. Aluna has a degree in Art History from Georgetown University in Washington, DC.

Ben Khan

Gallery Liaison

Ben's curiosities of the world contribute towards his motivations of constantly seeking fresh new perspectives of the art world. He received his Masters in Arts Administration and Cultural Policy from Goldsmiths, University of London.

Yuna Zarai

Senior Curator

Having an eye for a variety of art forms and a passion for writing, Aluna plays an active role in the art community by publishing research and information to journals, catalogs and books. She completed her BA (Hons) in History of Art from Goldsmiths, University of London, as well as holds a Masters in Contemporary Art Theory.
