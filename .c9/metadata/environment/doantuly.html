Doãn Tú Ly

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

Doãn (b. 1989 Da Nang, Vietnam) is a multi-hyphenate visual artist,\nwho has worked across a diverse breadth of mediums, motives and scale ranging from text,\nillustration, collage, light, sculpture to large scale installations. Her work primarily\ndeals with time, memory, identity and space. She studied studio art and journalism at\nGeorgetown University and the UCL Slade School of Fine Art and works across a breadth\nof media ranging from collage, photography, illustration, light, and installations.
